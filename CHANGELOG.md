# Telegram Downloader Changelog

## [Initial Version] - {PR_MERGE_DATE}

- New Download form with channel picker, media-type/date/size/caption/sender filters, output template, and resume options.
- Download Monitor menu-bar command with live progress, speed, and ETA.
- Quick Download from a link, @username, or the clipboard.
- Run Profile to launch saved tgdl profiles.
- Download History with per-channel stats and recent sessions.
- Manage Jobs to cancel, retry, open folders, and clear finished jobs.
